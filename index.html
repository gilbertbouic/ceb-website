Field ops for CEB Rodrigues
when the control room is far away.

CEB Rodrigues Field is an Android toolkit for generation monitoring, maintenance work orders, and structured export on the isolated Rodrigues grid — built for sites with intermittent connectivity. Complements mainland ADMS, SCADA, MoKouran and smart meters. It does not replace control-room systems.

Capabilities

Everything a field crew needs offline

Log generation, open work orders with photos, export reports, and optionally read local Modbus gateways — without waiting on the mainland WAN.

Generation dashboard

kWh by source with period filters: today, 7 days, 30 days, month, or all time. Built for Rodrigues local day (UTC+4).

Offline logging

Every write hits Room SQLite immediately. Correct mistakes in the field. Fully usable with no network.

Maintenance work orders

Create, filter, and move WOs through a status lifecycle. Attach camera or gallery photos on site.

Asset registry

Seeded for Pointe Monnier, Port Mathurin, Grenade, Trèfles wind, SSDG/MSDG pool, and more.

Share exports

CSV, JSON, and PDF via the Android share sheet for email, USB hand-off, or later aggregation.

Modbus TCP (optional)

Read-only holding registers from local gateways on LAN/VPN — never expose industrial protocols to the public internet.

How it works

From phone to structured report

  1. 1

    Install the field APK

    Side-load the signed pilot from this site (Android 8+). Operator profile auto-fills “recorded by”.

  2. 2

    Log generation & work

    Capture kWh by source and open maintenance tickets with photos while offline.

  3. 3

    Export or sync

    Share CSV/JSON/PDF now; background sync to a future edge API when connectivity returns.

Complements, does not replace

  • MoKouran — customer bills · this app is operator field O&M
  • ADMS / SCADA — central control · this app is offline site logging
  • Smart meters — customer metering · this app is source-level kWh + work orders

Designed for the isolated Rodrigues system, not as a mainland replacement stack.
